Rhode Island Maker Mill is a makerspace supporting the arts, engineering, and science in the Rhode Island and southern Massachusetts area. Our members are interested in all kinds of making including 3D printing, digital fabrication, electronics, fiber arts and more. We hold events, workshops and meet ups to learn, make and share.

COVID-19

Like the rest of the world COVID-19 has knocked us off our feet. We are just coming out of the slumber of isolation and have plans for online events, changes to our physical space and hopes to be working together in person soon.

Follow us on Instagram, Twitter and Facebook for updates.
